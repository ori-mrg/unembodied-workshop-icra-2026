About the Workshop

Bringing together academia and industry, we will examine architectures that blend onboard intelligence with edge/cloud services, with a focus on:

  • Real-time guarantees
  • Safety certification
  • Privacy and security
  • Human‚Äìrobot collaboration in factories, homes, hospitals, space and cities

The program features seven invited talks, rapid-fire poster pitches, and poster sessions for hands-on discussion. We conclude with awards for the best pitch and best poster, and a expert panel discussing the future of not fully embodied robotics.

Participation & Submissions

We encourage submissions of:

  • Abstracts presenting new ideas
  • Preliminary results or visionary concepts
  • Work based on prior publications (but not existing or under-review papers)

We welcome submissions from early-career and experienced researchers! This workshop is a venue for fresh, forward-looking research! All submissions will be reviewed for relevance and quality.
